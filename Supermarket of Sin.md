# Supermarket of Sin
* I'm in a busy supermarket.
* The atmosphere is frantic, depressing and stressful
* Everything on the shelves is trash, full of chemical additives, saturated fats, and carcinogens.
* People are so hungry, they spend everything they have on the rubbish and devour it.
* But it only makes them more hungry
* The managers in the stores and their boss over the company hate their customers and want them to not only die but make him rich in doing so.
## New Manager
* News reaches the store that the Founder of the company's Heir has taken over the company from the evil boss.
* He sends a representative into our store to change things.
* The new manager tells us about the new range of goods.
* He says there's already a deli counter and a fruit aisle, but almost noone goes there!
* Instead we need to replace the bad products that people are buying with better choices!
* Immediately, I go into a frenzy and start tearing products off the shelves.
* Before I know it, I'm waist deep in rubbish and, not only are the shelves automatically replenishing with the same rubbish, desperate customers are picking up rubbish products off the floor and buying them!
## New Products
* The new manager shows me how to add a new, healthy and affordable product to the front of the rack, pushing the queue of rubbish products back into the supply system.
* They tell me that the Founder is fixing the supply problem, but we also need to address the immediate problem of supply in each store.
* As we gradually replace all of the rubbish products, the whole atmosphere of the store changes from a desperate frenzy to a bright, calm, and peaceful one.
* Customers can afford to buy more of our better, cheaper, healthier products and don't have to shop as often so it's less crowded.
* They can see the staff restocking the shelves, so they're less anxious about things running out.
* Previous customers join the store as staff, some even making it as store managers after the example of the Founder and His Heir.
## Interpretation
* The rubbish, counterfeit products are sinful behaviours, inclinations, and habits.
* The malevelant figure running the company was the devil.
* The bad store managers were the Princes of the world, serving the devil.
* Satan was simultaneously killing us, employing us to kill others, and profiting off our misery.
* The Founder is God the Father, the Heir is God the Son, and the healthy, affordable, life giving product of them both is the fruit of God the Holy Spirit.
* The new managers are apostles and the staff are disciples.
* Through their good deeds, under the guidance of the apostles, the disciples restock the shelves with the fruit of the Holy Spirit.
