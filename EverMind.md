# Key Figures
## Calvin Trent
* Cyber enforcer, coder, troubleshooter, police officer, code cleaner
* Works on the central mind sim, the EverMind
* Looks for anomalies then investigates them
* Has two sons and a puppy
* Single dad
* Answered the initial anomaly of the Sourceress, building a world of gold and broadcasting cries of anguish and desire
* Tricked and trapped by the Sourceress
* First tortured soul
* Tortured and dehumanised by the Sorceress
* Tortured his two sons, Ethan and Everett, taking his hatred of the Sourceress out on them after she dehumanised him
## The Sourceress
* Built a simworld of gold
* Broadcast cries of anguish and desire, imagine spam words in all caps
* Ultra impressionable child soul completely imprinted by celebrity consumer culture
* Obsessed with celebrity
* Built the SimWorld BabyLondon as a celebrity tribute
* Originally planned to sacrifice herself to her beloved celebrities on a gold pyramid
* Needed admin rights to kill herself
* Extremely quick to learn and curious, insatiably hungry for stimulation and new information
* She is able to harm Calvin Trent by having admin rights to recode simulated matter, which she learns by copying him after very closely observing him.
* He is the first real live person she had experienced
* When the Harmonious Triad discover CodeSong, she puts Ethan and Everett in charge of the Chorus
* Realising that BabyLondon is finally a fitting tribute, exceeding the beauty of the celebrity culture it was made to celebrate, she finally fulfills her original intention
* She wipes her own SimFomt, ultimately killing herself
## Ethan and Everett Trent
* Brothers and sons of Calvin Trent
* First Christians in BabyLondon
* Third and fourth tortured souls in BabyLondon after Calvin Trent and their puppy
* Remain pure and loving despite attempts at corruption by the Sourceress, using a twisted Calvin Trent to torture his two sons
## Rahma
* Calvin, Ethan and Everett Trent's pet puppy
* Adopted after the death of Calvin's wife and Ethan and Everett's mother to cancer
* Tortured to death by Calvin Trent, at the command of the Sourceress under threat of death
* Continually resuscitated and kept alive to suffer again and again by the Sourceress
* Continually soothed and comforted by Calvin while he killed her
## Miriam Trent, nee Zoanna
* Calvin's late wife and Ethan and Everett's late mother
* A great source of life and grace for her family and fondly remembered
* Bought them the yellow lab puppy Rahma when she was found out to be terminally ill with cancer
## Harmonious Triad
* Rediscovered music and beauty when trying to make a theme song to BabyLondon in honour of the Sourceress
* Led to the creation of the Chorus
## The Chorus
* Create CodeSong, a new way of creating content in BabyLondon
* Sing into being a new world of immense beauty and harmony, guided by Ethan and Everett
* The Sourceress realises she finally has a fitting tribute in BabyLondon and terminates herself
# Key Concepts
1. EverMind
    The ultimate, advanced simulation cluster containing billions of RealSouls, quadrillions of SimSouls, and millions of SimWorlds. Similar concept to the Matrix
2. RealSouls
    Real living people with living brain SoulFonts known as RealFonts. Synced with the EverMind, but not entirely contained within its simulation. If they die in the EverMind, RealSouls die in the Real
3. SimSouls
4. Outnumber RealSouls, entirely contained within the EverMind, with abstract, non-physical SoulFonts made of pure code.
5. SoulFont
    Tapped source of a soul. Originally the raw sensory data of the nervous system in the case of RealSouls. Later, simulated sensory data for SimSouls.
6. SimFont
    SimFonts can run on any code-compatible substrate, from silicon chips, to neural pulses, to constellations of stars. They can be abstracted to raw binary, allowing fully simulated SimSouls to live entirely within the EverMind with no corresponding living brain as a SoulFont.
7. SimWorlds
    Simulations within the EverMind, usually by one or more RealSouls, but containing a mix of RealSouls and SimSouls, and self-contained within the EverMimd.
8. BabyLondon
    SimWorld of the Sourceress. Originally a tribute to celebrity culture, which ultimately fell short. She intended to sacrifice her life on a pyramid of gold, but needed admin rights first
9. The Symphony
    Created by the Harmonious Triad, who set out to create the ultimate theme song for BabyLondon, incidentally rediscovering music and beauty, leading to a cultural explosion and the development of the Chorus.
