I was placed in a flying sphere, controlled by my thinking in the direction I wanted to go in. I was shown the past and future of a parallel world, seeing the future that God had engineered for us. My species were part of a symbiosis between a humanoid species and at least two insectoid species. I was tasked with bringing my revelations down without ruining the existing balance. Something about the situation filled me with a deep sense of dread.

I closed the ship's hatch in the face a beautiful humanoid male climbing a ladder. Privately, I decided to step into the filth of the world. Perhaps, I wasn't meant to.

I described myself having spoken to the God of the Four Skies, or a being imperceptibly different. Then I woke up.
