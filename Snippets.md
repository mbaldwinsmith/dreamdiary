# Hugs
*   Lucid dream in a city market
*   brushing my hands over surfaces to stay anchored in the dream
*   followed a fluorescent sign saying HUGS which pointed to a line of friendly-looking cowled monks near a large red-brick building

# going for silver
*   long pillared gallery full of stone slabs with dead senior church leaders in glittering robes lying on them
*   many of the rather bloated, gaudy looking figures wore extravagant gold and bejewelled crucifixes.
*   every time I reached out to touch a gold crucifix, it turned black and crumbled
*   around my own neck was a simple silver crucifix
*   I felt strongly told not to go for the gold of high ecclesiastic office

# editors
*   black crackling light
*   Insects. Clicking noise. Invisible. Editors. Conquerors. Tornado.
*   black-robed figure with a black light sabre crackling with electricity and blackened veins and pale skin.
*   black-robed figure pointed straight at me.
*   Figures of light, rank after rank, lying prostrate before an enthroned figure of black.
*   A preoccupation with insectoid, machine life and people living with rigid, binary belief systems, rendered insectoid or machine-like in the simplicity of their dualism. Light Vs dark similar to binary code.
*   Primacy of the Law above all things, perhaps even God. Similar to Jainism.
