# Monkey Junkie
* In a Norse town with monkey vikings
* Big viking has a younger Viking attached to his chest on a plank
* The younger monkey viking is given a hallucinogen
* They are also making sacrifices of people and animals
* Dylan was there
* We asked where the nearest Christian priest was and they said overseas
* When the younger monkey was untethered, an older scared and jealous monkey sliced his thigh with a knife to test him
* We tried to stop them but they threatened us
* The young monkey woke up, gave a speech, grabbed a sword and chased after the older monkey viking
* They both jumped over a large wire fence
* For the first time, I noticed lights on the other side
* The police arrived, triggered by the monkeys jumping over the fence
* Dylan and I explained what happened, explaining that we couldn't remember how we got there.
* There were two policeman, one big and polish, the other smaller and English
* I told the polish policeman that I was a Catholic, and he told me that he was an atheist Catholic.
* We had a long conversation and I shared my testimony
