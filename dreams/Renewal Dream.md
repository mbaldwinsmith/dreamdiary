I dreamt that I was in a city or large town, staying in a Church. The Church generated its own energy, had a lively social presence, and even had its own swimming pool. I walked into the swimming pool room without getting changed or showering. Somebody shouted, "A Knight muddied the floor without washing his feet". This happened twice. I took it to be referring to me entering the House of God while unclean. I was tramping everyone else's muck in, with no thought to the children who were having their lessons or baptisms in the swimming pool. Rather than confessing, I stayed silent, rationalising that my feet were much much cleaner than many peoples'. The sinner's prayer would have been more fitting!
