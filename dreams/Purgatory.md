# Purgatory
* In a liminal space resembling Nana and Granddad's house and garden
* Mum, dad, nan, granddad and others were there
* Spoke to Nan, sitting down next to her near the shed, as she sat next to granddad
* Asked her if she was in Paradise
* She answered no sadly
* Asked her if she was in purgatory
* She said yes
* She veiled herself
* I asked how long she had left
* She said 6 years
* I said that wasn't too long
* She complained that it was long
* She also complained about someone, possibly a demon, saying that they'd had sex with her sister, and that her sister was only 16
* She seemed confused
* I encouraged her that these were lies and that me and Mother Mary were praying for her
* She also said that she kept forgetting to study
* I asked her if she had a rosary, showing her mine and telling her the story, which she loved
* She showed me hers, but it was all tangled and broken, possibly indicating that souls in purgatory can't pray for themselves
* I also spoke to dad, who said the people weren't so bad
