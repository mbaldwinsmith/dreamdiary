# Heavenly Ascension
## Lucid garden dream
I was in a big house and realised I was dreaming. Grooveshark played on my PC, giving things away. I went downstairs and into the garden, just knowing my way.

Everything felt very real. I went for a swim in a stream and could breath under water. While under water, I started saying the Jesus Prayer!

## Ascension
I was carried up into the heavens, surrounded by a golden circle of symbols and faces, beautiful song and an image of Jesus, carrying me in His arms. I tingled all over, and felt an elated feeling, like living water, course through my body.

## Heavenly chambers
I went into a gothic citadel with Jesus, guarded by some armoured figures. Some of the armoured figures would go for me, recognising me as an unclean outsider, but Jesus would command them to stop. The citadel was beautiful, gilded and glittering.

## Back in the garden
I found myself back in the garden with Kelly, who quizzed me about my swim. I explained that I had been lucid dreaming, but I appear to have been back in the dream by then!
