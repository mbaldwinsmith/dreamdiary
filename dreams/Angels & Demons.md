# Angels & Demons
* Spiritual beings that interact with human beings
* Different orders
* Some delight in the physical world
* Others loathe it and the beings in it
* Believe in the Source, Logos-Sophia, and Presence.
* Some embarrassed by the name Jesus, others scared of it, while others love it
* Different factions, not simply good and evil


## Sub plot
* Human detective
* Specialises in the occult, eldritch, ritualistic, and religious
* Works closely with the Catholic Church
* Research exorcisms and the types of crimes committed
* Married
* Comes home one day to find her husband limp with pleasure, exhausted and sleeping
* A stunningly beautiful man is standing over him, glistening with sweat
* She is enchanted by him
* He starts seducing her, only to be interrupted by someone squealing "Archon, no!"
* Turns out they're very closely related and both the beautiful man and the "human" detective are human-angel hybrids of the same order
* She has forgotten her origin
## Varieties of Angelic Experience
* Some angels choose to live in the burning cores of stars for billions of years with millions of other angels, where they dwell in almost flawless spiritual perfection
* Other angels choose the desolate nothing-wastes at the edge of the known Universe, where they can almost entirely escape physical matter
* Yet other angels delight in physical matter, inhabiting trees, or roaring torrents, or mountains, or treasured belongings
* Higher angels may delight in flesh, in sex, food, drugs, extreme sports, art, music, culture, war, etc.
* Holy angels protect and guide people, participate in wars on the side of the prevailing good, guide earthly affairs, and convict people's consciences.
* Holy angels protect, especially, against fallen angels and demons, or even just those curious with human life, which can be deadly and deeply corrupting in themselves
