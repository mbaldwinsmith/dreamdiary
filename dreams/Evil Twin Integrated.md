# Evil Twin Integrated
* Faced my evil Twin, who mocked and laughed at me
* Fought back, chasing him around
* Became lucid and stretched my arms out to catch him. Stood still, but my arms chased him around, but never got closer to him as he moved away at the same pace
* He cackled maniacally and shouted that I could never defeat him as he exists in relation to me, as part of me
* I realised this was true and stopped chasing him
* Instead, I calmed my breathing and focussed on absorbing him into my being, letting my emotions and attachments pass me by
* He disappeared and I felt whole
* I woke up
