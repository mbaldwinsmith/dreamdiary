# Church Event
* In a huge auditorium, bigger than C3, like a MegaChurch, with mum and dad
* It was a bawdy comedy show
* They had people planted in the audience, who would gradually make themselves known and participate in the show
* Eventually, almost everyone except my parents and I in the Auditorium were part of the show
# Hacking Dad
* They hacked my Dad's phone and called him on video chat
* They called him as a funeral director, it came up on the screen, and started reciting things he'd been watching/listening to
* His phone screen came up on the big screen
* It spliced between different calls and podcasts about God or Jesus, building up a case against him, asserting that he was pretty much a closet Christian
* It showed all the Christian content he'd consumed, he shouted and fumed saying "leave me alone!"
# The Field
* I was suddenly in a field in a robe, holding a mass of stuff in my hands
* A black dog attacked my dad and then me
* I shouted out something like "Lord do something, save him"
* I saw a procession of people in robes holding assorted objects over their heads like garlands, shouting "Jesus is Lord!"
* I joined the procession, holding aloft the mass of stuff in my hands, proclaiming "Jesus is Lord"
* I followed the procession for a while, until someone turned and interrogated me "BUT WHOSE LORD IS HE!?", I shouted back "MY LORD, JESUS IS MY LORD!"
* I kept chanting and the forest fell away, I was lifted up into the sky, surrounded by golden light and a beautiful scene with a chorus of angels all singing in harmony, "Jesus is Lord!"
# The Confrontation
* I found myself back on the ground, with my family, who were scared and concerned for me
* I tried to reassure them and told them "Jesus is Lord!"
* They turned on me, getting increasingly irrational and angry
* I told them "the devil is real" and they calmed down and agreed
* I repeated, "Jesus is real", "God is real" and their faces contorted aggressively
* I became aggressive in return, which only made it worse
* I repeated "the devil is real" to try and calm them down again
* I desperately panicked and thought of ways to convince them, but nothing worked
* Notably, I didn't pray and stopped saying "Jesus is Lord, for fear of angering them
# The Depression Injection
* I was sat on a couch, surrounded by them and two strangers, a menacing man and woman in black clothes
* "We've talked to the doctor and your vicar and they agree, you should get the depression injection" they said
* The menacing woman opened her mouth, revealing a stinking mouth full of needles. She lunged at me with her needle-mouth but I pushed the man in the way and she bit his nose
* I got up and ran, lost, then woke up and prayed
# The Prayer
* Beloved Father, thank for this dream and for a glimpse of your throne. Thank you for the evidence that my Dad has indeed been feeling his way to faith, and that he still has blockages. Sorry for failing my family and you in stopping proclaiming that you're Lord for fear of angering them. Sorry for resting upon my own limited understanding of You and them, in attempting to convince them, instead of calling upon You. Please, in Your mercy, do no abandon me to them, but equally do not abandon them without me, because sometimes it seems like I'm the only light and chance they have. In your boundless mercy, please save them as You have me, but equally, do not let me endanger my salvation for fear of them. Heaven and Earth are Yours, and Jesus is truly Lord of all. I pray this in His beautiful and boundless name! Amen.
# Megan's interpretation
So the fact your in a bigger auditorium could represent the wider world outside of church? Like with your mum and dad- could show the religious application outside of C3 setting and that gap that God has created for you to sow the word of God outside in this other opportunity. Maybe it isn’t the way you see it at face value, and there is a bigger meaning in everything you’ve been hearing. 2 Corinthians 4:18- there is more in what we don’t see that what we see at face value.
So the people gradually participating- people all come to Christ at different timings, and the emphasis of the word ‘Gradually’ highlights that it is Gods timing not ours. The fact it left you and your parents could represent the fact that there is something unresolved between you guys to come to that PEACE that the others have come to when fully committing to this ‘show’. God is saying, take a step back about your assumpsions of this ‘show’ and just let yourself all COLLECTIVELY participate. We are all children of God, Jesus died on the cross for all of us- so we can ALL step forward in our role as stewards and living in harmony.
I feel like the ‘hacking’ of your Dad is almost God directly saying something here. I don’t know your families current religious status or circumstances- so forgive me if I am being guided in the wrong direction, but I feel like theres real movement coming somewhere in your family. The fact many DIFFERENT sources are coming up is showing that God is working in ways in your Dads life more than you can imagine, and he’s almost silently becoming more and more influenced- without wanting to be admitted. Maybe this is a dig at humility for you- and that it is something you need to work on? And your father? Isaiah 55:8-9- There is a difference between God’s actions and plans which are far beyond our comprehension- which we might not want to recognise at the moment. There’s going to be some sort of event/season of your life where it will feel rocky for your guys relationship, and it will feel MEGA uncomfortable- but it’s going to lead to a conviction.
Field of the Lord- Many say they’ve seen God in dreams in a field- maybe this is him drawing closer to you? So the fact you’ve both been bitten by a dog is significant. In Ancient times, dogs were metaphorically used in several passages to represent uncleanliness. This shows that currently in your life your facing this uncleanliness from one area, and God is showing you very VIVIDLY. At the end it is BEAUTIFUL with Angels, God has got you in the end despite this, despite the chaos, despite the uncleanliness in your life- you are SAVED. He died on the cross. And even when others may judge your lifestyle around you (even fellow christians)- God will always have you
The Confrontation- This is a period of PANIC and confrontation. The dynamic of this shows the constant weight game, where we have to do things just to please others and stop THEIR irrationality. Don’t stop your praise of God to stop peoples anger. Although it may be their irrationality, we will always face chaos for choosing JESUS- but we need to remain persistant.
Depression injection-
This violence at the end is a representation of your mind when facing these challenges with your family. Your fearing rejection, anger and hostility, in response to your actions, and your running away from this violence. Injections almost represent the constant slipping in of the word, and this SCARES you as your scared your family are going to become hostile. You ran away ‘lost’- which shows, this way of living does not affirm direction, but insteads adds further layers to this.
