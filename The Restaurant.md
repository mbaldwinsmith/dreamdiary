# The Restaurant
I had a lucid dream in which I was in a beautiful city, reading advertising, touching walls and just taking it in. I tried to goto the Grafton centre and instead found a huge mixed gothic and contemporary structure.

I found a small restaurant and ordered a steak, but it was taking too long to arrive so I left. I went to the loo, which had strange bamboo mini compartments. I did a wee in the dream, but the urine fired back onto my penis. I didn't wet myself in real life. I also needed a bowel movement in the dream but didn't when I woke up.

I stirred from the dream, which had no narrative. I jumped back into it at one point when I recognised that I was lying in unfamiliar surroundings.

Words kept changing on closer observation. I kept rubbing my hands together or on surfaces to keep my senses immersed in the dream.

At some point some one created a puppet show by transforming a wagon.
